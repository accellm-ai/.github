# Accellm

**Production-grade AI systems for ambitious teams**

Accellm helps startups and enterprises design, build, and operationalize AI systems that create measurable business value. We work across agentic workflows, LLM infrastructure, and custom AI products to turn promising ideas into reliable production systems.

## What We Do

### AI Agents & Agentic Workflows
We design and implement agent-based systems that automate repetitive work, streamline operations, and improve team productivity. This includes workflow design, tool integration, orchestration, and human-in-the-loop systems for high-trust use cases.

### AI Platform & LLM Infrastructure
We build the technical foundation required to ship reliable AI applications. That includes LLM platforms, RAG systems, evaluation pipelines, observability, deployment workflows, and scalable backend infrastructure for production AI.

### Custom AI Products
We help companies create AI-native products and intelligent features tailored to their business. From applied machine learning systems to domain-specific copilots and internal knowledge tools, we build solutions that align with real customer and operational needs.

## How We Work

We focus on practical AI engineering with an emphasis on:

- **Production readiness** — built for real users, real scale, and real operational constraints
- **Measurable quality** — evaluation, testing, and monitoring are part of the system
- **Secure-by-default design** — careful handling of data, access, and deployment boundaries
- **Business alignment** — technical decisions tied to actual outcomes, not demo value alone
- **Human-centered workflows** — automation where it helps, human oversight where it matters
